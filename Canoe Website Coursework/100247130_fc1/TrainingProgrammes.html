Gaining

Qualifications

The qualifications you can gain from doing canoeing are:

Qualifications Description Price
Canoeing 1 Star The British Canoeing 1 Star Award is where you master some basic skills in kayak and canoe. You will develop skills such as Launching and landing, forwards paddling and turning and looking at equipment & safety. The course will also involve a coastal journey (2 Days) £165
Canoeing 2 Star This is an improver award and once completed the paddler will have developed a better understanding of how the paddle, boat and water interact. The course is predominantly practical but there is also an element of theory. PLEASE NOTE the course will assess both Kayaking and Open Canoeing and you need to be competent in both (2 Days) £165 – Prerequisite Canoeing 1 Star
Canoeing 3 Star Sea Training This course will cover in-depth the whole of the 3 star syllabus and we will use a variety of locations to get the optimal conditions to test out your skills. Each day will involve a journey of approximately 16km in moderate tidal conditions and where possible moderate wind and wave conditions (up to force 4) (2 Days) £165 – Prerequisite Canoeing 2 Star
Canoeing 3 Star Sea Assessment This is the level requires for entry into the British Canoeing coaching courses and as such candidates are required to demonstrate a high level of ability of the 3 star syllabus. The day will involve a journey around the coast looking at the skills required for a moderate tidal environment with winds up to force 4. (1 Day) £85 – Prerequisite Canoeing 3 Star Sea Training
Canoeing Sea Leader Training (4 Star) This course aims to teach you the skills required to lead a group of 4 people in moderate tidal waters and in winds up to force 4. Upon completion, you will be given a detailed action plan for progressing to the 4 star assessment. (2 Days) £165 – Prerequisite Canoeing 3 Star Sea
Canoeing Sea Leader Assessment (4 Star) Prerequisite – Canoeing 4 Star Sea Training, a first aid award, logged experience. (2 Days) £165 + Reg Fee
