The Clubhouse

The club has two newly built, state of the art clubhouse. The clubhouse incorporates a room dedicated to only paddling and rowing machines. Both clubhouses have male and female changing facilities and also has separate kitchen, bar, office and on-site physiotherapy facilities for both clubhouses.
