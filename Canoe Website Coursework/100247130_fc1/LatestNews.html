Become a Personal Performance Awards Provider

Canary Canoe are pleased to announce that every Instructor, Coach, Leader, Raft Guide and Bell Boat Helm will be able to deliver the NEW Personal Performance Awards, providing greater access for the paddling community.

All Providers will need to complete the Provider eLearning package before delivering the Personal Performance Awards. This online package can be accessed in your own time, reducing the need for travel, making this a more cost-effective route for all Providers. The eLearning takes approximately an hour to complete.

The provider eLearning was useful to gain the information about the new awards and allowed me to have my questions answered with the added links and support videos.

Weekend in America

Sea Kayak Rendezvous was held in Maine between October 6th - 8th with a great turnout and much enthusiasm associated with the latest Canary Canoe developments.

Alongside the annual Rendezvous, the first Sea Kayak Coach assessments in North America took place, as well as the first Paddlesport Instructor Provider Orientation, run by Lianne Diane. The first providers to be oriented were Jen Cleck of Los Angeles, Don Cheyette of Chicago and Andrea Knepper from New York.

Lennie Theodore presented the Canary Canoe update, with a great response to everything coming from Canary Canoe.

NEW Awards!

The NEW Personal Performance Awards launch in January 2019 and are designed for paddlers wanting to gain recognition of their learning and development, in the craft and environment they choose.

Over the next month, we'll be sharing information about the new awards so keep an eye on the British Canoeing website or find out more about the NEW Personal Performance Awards on the 'New for 2019' section.
