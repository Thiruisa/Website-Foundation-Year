Facilities

We have many facilities available:

  • Changing Rooms - Changing rooms are provided for both male and female members
  • Showers - Separate shower rooms are also provided for both male and female members
  • Secure Storage Room - The clubhouse provides a secure storage rooms for member to place their personal belongings
  • Canoe and Kayak equipment - Canoe and Kayak equipment are provided for newcomers who don't already have their own. However, members should bring their own, if forgetten we always have spare available for loan for a small charge
  • Lecture Rooms - There are lecture rooms in both of our clubhouses, where coaches teach about safety and other important information needed
